# Installation Guide for MS Project Desktop
---

<ins>**IMPORTANT POINT 1:**</ins>  You will be given **specific credentials** for a new Microsoft account in order to install the MS Project Desktop app **(Please DO NOT use your original Microsoft Account to sign in)**.

<ins>**IMPORTANT POINT 2:**</ins>  Please **DO NOT** use any Remote Desktop function, and follow the process locally on your PC/Laptop. 

<ins>**IMPORTANT POINT 3:**</ins>  For step 1, please use an **Incognito window** on your preferred browser **(Please DO NOT use a standard browser window)**.

---

1. Open an **Incognito window** on your browser and go to **[www.office.com](www.office.com)** and select **"Sign in"**.

   <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MSProject%201.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


2. Sign in with the account credentials **that were provided to you** (Note that the first time you will log in with the account, you will be asked to change your password).


3. After signing in to Microsoft 365 click on upper right corner **“Install apps”** (“Εγκατάσταση Εφαρμογών”) and click on the next choice **“Other install options”**

   <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MS%20Project%202.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


4. In the next screen select **“Apps and devices”** (“Προβολή εφαρμογών και συσκευών”)

   <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MS%20Project%203.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


5. In the next screen click on **“Install Project”**

   <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MS%20Project%204.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


6. Run the OfficeSetup.exe that was downloaded. The installer will install only the Project App. The rest of your Office 365 apps will be unaffected. 


7. After the installation ends, press the Start button on your keyboard, and the **Project** app will show up on top. Open the app and sign in with the account credentials **that were provided to you**.

    <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MS%20Project%205.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


8. In the next screen please **MAKE SURE** to click on **“No, sign in to this app only”**.

    <kbd><img src="https://github.com/codehub-learn/development-environment-setup/blob/main/images/MS%20Project%206.PNG" width="600" height="auto"> </kbd>


9. Enjoy a 1 month trial for MS Project Server!
